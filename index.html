About

"Writing has always been my first love."

I started with an English Literature degree in India, writing ad scripts and copy for agencies on the side. When I realised I was as much a strategist and visualiser as a writer, I did something about it: MA Advertising and Design at the University of Leeds.

Since then I have scripted television commercials for national banks and global NGOs, led website projects across EMEA, built email programmes that moved measurable numbers, and managed marketing for one of the UK's largest student communities.

I work at the intersection of creative and strategic. Comfortable writing an ad script one day and presenting a data-backed content audit the next. That range is not a lack of focus. It is the point.

Currently based in Manchester. Open to mid to senior marketing, content strategy, and project leadership roles across the UK.
