NGO and Purpose Films
CSR FilmICICI Foundation x Sariska Tiger Reserve
2024 +

Told through the eyes of ST9, a tiger at Sariska Tiger Reserve. Shot across tough terrain, the film captures the resilience of the reserve and its wildlife. ICICI Foundation, in collaboration with the Rajasthan Government and Forest Department, implemented water conservation, grassland augmentation, enhanced ranger resources, and livelihood support for forest dwellers across 56 forests and tiger reserves in India. Script, concept, casting, costume, and post-production edits.
